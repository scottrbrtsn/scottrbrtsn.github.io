# Rent or own?

## Your house
The most obvious comparison.  

Most investments into real estate turn out better in the long run. 

## Your clothes
This is weird.


## Your transportation
Rent a taxi or an uber. 

Own a car.

## Your compute
SaaS rents from the cloud.  


## Your employee
A paycheck is a rental agreement.  

Ownership of employees, feels like slavery. 

But employees owning their company...feels like something else.

## A company
If I buy something, I rent a piece of the company. 

If I invest, I own part of the company.


## Your government
We pay taxes, so technically, we rent our property and citizenship from the government.

What would it look like to own the government?
