In the darkness I lay my head,

The night sky above shines bright as lead,

Stars twinkle like diamonds in the void,

A universe beyond our human mood,

Winds howl through the trees outside,

Leaves rustling softly in reply,

Branches creak and groan in protest,

As if they feel the weight of time pass by,

Raindrops patter on the window glass,

Like tears from heaven falling fast,

Thunder rumbles deep within the earth,

A symphony that echoes forth,

Sunbeams pierce the morning mist,

Illuminating all that we behold,

Colors dance upon the canvas wide,

A tapestry of life unrolled,

Transcendent beauty in each sight,

Suffering lurks beneath the surface bright,

But in this moment we find release,

Embrace the world and all it brings,
