# The geometry of ideas

## A point 
Solipsism.  
I am right
Nothing else matters.

There exists no justice.
The authoritarian tyrant craving an imaginary crown.
Good luck.

## 2 points
Good/Evil
I am right. 
You are wrong.
Or, the grass is always greener.

There exists justice.
The moralist/legalist craving power and prestige as judge and jury.
Good luck convincing the solitary point the multiplicative nature of reality...

## A line
A spectrum
Context matters. 
We can meet in the middle. 
Except when you only consider yourself as a point. Then no matter where I land, I'm the extreme point of view contrary to your own. 

There exists forgiveness.
The contextual intellectual craving wisdom among the context
Good luck convincing binary points the continual nature of reality...

## A tree


Good luck convincing a line the hierarchical nature of reality.

## A circle
Extremes
Too far left, you end up back at the beginning.
Sysyphus finds himself either at the bottom or the top of the hill.  

There exists awareness.
The wise sage, craving a way out from that which is seen, knowing something cannot be seen is beyond, 
Good luck convincing the tree eternal nature of reality... 

## A helix 
Like a 3D spiral (e.g. springs, DNA)
Progress 
Growth
Transcendent

There exists faith.
The monk, the yogi, craving an encounter, or union with the divine, or to simply serve.
Good luck convincing the circle the 3D nature of reality... 

## Empty space

There exists the infinite.
Good luck convincing the helix the omnidirectional nature of reality. 

## Time

There exists the eternal.
Good luck convincing empty space, the dynamically, impermanent nature of reality.

## God

There, existence is emergent.
Good luck convincing time, the extratemporal nature of reality.
